Celebs Don't Care

How much do celebrities pollute with their jets?

Purpose

After looking at this post from Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott, it makes you think if you are really making a change. You know? I stopped using straws, did it matter? Well... maybe not as much as I think but I am helping even if it's the smallest of changes. Either way, how much are celebrities are polluting? Well we can find out using this tool.

Tool

Data gathered is displayed to show how bad celebrities are damaging the world. It makes comparisons to show their true impact. Filter and find out.

Credits

Built with Go, PostgreSQL, jQuery.
